##### Instructions to make Taco Pizza:

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

1. Top with hamburger and cheese evenly.
1. Bake in oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
1. Top with desired toppings.
1. Enjoy!
