  4. Prepare Onion
  5. Prepare 3/4 Cup Pasta or Pizza Sauce
  6. Get 1/4 Can Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 Cups Cheese (Grated)
  8. Prepare Parmesan Cheese (Grated)
  9. Get Parsley

Steps to make Pizza Toast:
  1. Preheat oven to 450 F. Slice bread in half lengthwise. Chop and dice veggies. Chop Chipotle peppers and mix a couple tablespoons of adobo sauce into pizza sauce. Spoon or brush sauce onto bread. Distribute cheese evenly amongst both slices. Layer on salami followed by the veggies. Lastly, grate Parmesan over the top.
  2. Place in a preheated oven for 7-10 minutes. Check it after 7 minutes to see if it’s done to your liking. Sprinkle some parsley and oregano on it to make it official. Cut bread in half again for four servings.
