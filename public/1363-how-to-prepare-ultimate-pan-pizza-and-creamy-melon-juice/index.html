The ingredients needed to make Pan Pizza and Creamy Melon Juice:
  1. Prepare For Pan Pizza
  2. Make ready 6 White Loaf Bread
  3. Take 4 slices cooked ham, cut in small cubes
  4. Prepare 1 Quickmelt cheese, grated about ¾ of the bar
  5. Prepare 115 grams Del Monte Sweet Style Pizza Sauce
  6. Make ready 1 cup (227 g) pineapple tidbits
  7. Take For Creamy Melon Juice
  8. Take 1 Medium Melon
  9. Prepare 1 can (154 ml) Evaporated Milk
  10. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  11. Take 4 cups water
  12. Take cube Ice

Steps to make Pan Pizza and Creamy Melon Juice:
  1. For Pan Pizza: - Using a teaspoon, spoon pizza sauce over each bread equally and spread it.
  2. Equally distribute cubed hams and pieces of pineapple tidbits.
  3. Finish off with a final layer of grated cheese.
  4. On a non-stick pan, place your pizza, put cover and cook over extra low heat.
  5. When the cheese melted, your pizza is ready. Remove from heat. Serve hot.
  6. For Creamy Melon Juice: - Slice the melon into half. Remove all the seeds.
  7. Using a melon scraper, scrape the melon and place in a large bowl. Set aside.
  8. In a large pitcher, combine water, sugar, and milk. Stir until the sugar is diluted. Add the shredded melon. Stir again.
  9. Put lots of ice. Serve. - NOTE: If using milk you need to consume it immediately as it will turn bitter due to their proteolytic enzyme which breaks down the milk proteins
