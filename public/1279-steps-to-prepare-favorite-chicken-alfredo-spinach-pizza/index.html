The ingredients needed to make Chicken Alfredo Spinach Pizza:
  1. Make ready 1 packages Pillsbury pizza dough(or homemade) :)
  2. Take 2 cup shredded cooked chicken
  3. Take 2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. Make ready 1 can Alfredo Sauce (I made my own)
  5. Prepare 2 cup drained canned or frozen spinach
  6. Take 1 tsp olive oil
  7. Prepare 1 cup sliced mushrooms

Be sure to sit down at the. This pizza turned out pretty good if you want a little pizza variety. I like to make my own Alfredo Sauce using light laughing cow cheese and half and half, instead Top the crust with cooked spinach. Then spread chicken mixture over the spinach.

Instructions to make Chicken Alfredo Spinach Pizza:
  1. preheat oven to 345º
  2. Roll out pizza dough into a circle shape
  3. Put pizza dough on pizza stone or pizza pan
  4. (Optional) spread the olive oil along the edges of pizza
  5. Spread 3/4 of the can of Alfredo sauce on pizza dough
  6. Spread out two cups shredded chicken evenly
  7. pour sliced mushrooms along the pizza
  8. Spread drained spinach onto pizza dough
  9. Then take the mozzarella and spread it along over the spinach covering completely
  10. Put in oven to bake 30-35 mins
  11. let cool for 10 mins
  12. (Optional)Then take the remaining Alfredo sauce put in Piping or Ziploc bag cut corner and drizzle lines along the pizza for decorating
  13. Now ready to serve( with side salad or bread sticks!)
