Instructions to make Fake-pizza birthday cake:
  1. Mix all the ingredients for the cake except milk
  2. Slowly add milk being careful to stir well
  3. When homogeneous pour in a greased pan and bake at 150¬∞C for 20 minutes
  4. To prepare the filling put dry gelatine leaves in cold water until they soften, then squeeze and let them melt in warm milk
  5. Add evaporated milk and whipped cream
  6. put filling in the fridge for 1 hour
  7. Add chocolate chips and your filling is ready
  8. Cut cake into two layers and evenly spread the filling on the first one, then close with the other one
  9. Crumb coat the whole cake with whipped cream or jam or buttercream
  10. Use yellow/orange sugar paste to make the pizza crust and red one for the tomato sauce
  11. Cut marshmallows to make the mozzarella slices and glue them to the sugar paste using edible glue
  12. Use green sugar paste to make the basil leaves and brown sugar paste to make the sausage's slices
