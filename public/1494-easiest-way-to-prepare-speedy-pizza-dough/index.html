The ingredients needed to make Pizza dough:
  1. Take 2.5 c all purpose flour
  2. Take .5 c wheat flour
  3. Get 1/3 c olive oil
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp yeast
  5. Make ready 1 c water
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  7. Get 1 tbsp salt
  8. Prepare spray oil

Instructions to make Pizza dough:
  1. Proof the yeast : microwave the water to Luke warm then add the sugar and the yeast and mix until frothy
  2. Add flour
  3. Make a little well for the salt - add salt and cover since the yeast can't touch the salt
  4. Add olive oil and mix with fork
  5. Split in half and knead
  6. Freeze for later or add sauce and cook
  7. Bake at 450 for. 20 min
