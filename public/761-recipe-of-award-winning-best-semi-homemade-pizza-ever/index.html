Steps to make Best semi-homemade pizza EVER!:
  1. Pre heat oven to 450
  2. If stored in refrigerator allow to come to room temp on kitchen counter. Place dough on 18 inch pizza pan. I prefer a pan which allows the bottom of crust to crisp(the ones with holes on the bottom).
  3. With your fingertips press down quickly, the inside of the pizza dough to outside where you want your crust to begin. The purpose is to stretch the dough enough to into a circle that you can now pick up and move around the inside of the dough… stretching to make thinner allowing a more restaurant style pizza.
  4. Now, ladel/spoon as little (my preference) or as much pizza sauce as you like with getting any on the crust zone. Next, add cheese and top with pepperoni.
  5. Place pizza inside and bake till geese melts. Slice and enjoy with your family! It taste just like Empire's NYC style pizza! Except a large didn't cost $25 and I made it myself!
