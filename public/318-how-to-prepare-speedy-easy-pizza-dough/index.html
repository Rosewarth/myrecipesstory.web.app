The ingredients needed to make easy pizza dough:
  1. Prepare 3 cup plain flour
  2. Make ready 7 1/2 grams dried yeast
  3. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 10 ml olive oil
  5. Make ready 100 ml warm water

Instructions to make easy pizza dough:
  1. pre heat oven to max temp
  2. mix flour, yeast and salt in a mixing bowl
  3. add oil to warm water
  4. make a well in the middle of flour mix. slowly add water and stir in with 2 fingers
  5. when you have a dough knees then cover until doubles in size
  6. cut dough to required size and roll out
