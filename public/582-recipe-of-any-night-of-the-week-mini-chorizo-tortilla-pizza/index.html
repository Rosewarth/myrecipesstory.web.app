The ingredients needed to make Mini Chorizo Tortilla Pizza üçï:
  1. Make ready 1 pack Chorizo (16 ounce), remove from casing
  2. Make ready 1 onion, sliced
  3. Make ready 1/2 green pepper, sliced
  4. Make ready 2 packs (6 ounce) fresh mushrooms, sliced
  5. Get 6 cloves garlic, minced
  6. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  7. Get 1/4 teaspoon Goya adobo
  8. Make ready 2 cans (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes
  9. Make ready 1 can (5 ounce) black olives, drained
  10. Get 1 pack Goya Mini Corn tortillas
  11. Prepare Shredded Mexican cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Mini Chorizo Tortilla Pizza üçï:
  1. Place chorizo into a large skillet, cut into coins and heat on medium heat……
