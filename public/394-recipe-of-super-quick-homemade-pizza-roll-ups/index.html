Instructions to make pizza roll ups:
  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. separate crescent rolls starting at the bigger end line up 3 slices of pepperoni
  3. cut string cheese in half place on pepperoni
  4. then roll crescents starting at the big end roll all the way to tip
  5. place in oven for 10 to13 mins..
  6. enloy dip in sauce of your choosing my choice is ranch
