The ingredients needed to make Pampered Chef's Cool Vegi Pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese
  2. Get 1 can flat cressent roll dough
  3. Get 1 tsp dill weed
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  5. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  6. Prepare Any diced vegetables you like
  7. Make ready 1 clove garlic pressed

Steps to make Pampered Chef's Cool Vegi Pizza:
  1. Roll out your cressent roll dough onto a baking sheet or pizza pan and bake for 13 to 15 min on 375°
  2. While this bakes set out the cream cheese to soften, and dice your vegetables. We used carrot, broccoli, cucumber, mushroom, peppers, green onion, and spinach leaves.
  3. In a bowl mix the softened cream cheese, mayonnaise, dill, garlic, salt and pepper.
  4. Once the crust is baked and cooled spread on the cream cheese mixture, and top with vegetable's.
  5. Note: The base for this pizza makes a great dip for your vegetables if you want to stay away from the carbs, just skip the crust.
