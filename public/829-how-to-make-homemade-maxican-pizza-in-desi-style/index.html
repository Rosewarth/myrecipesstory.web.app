The ingredients needed to make Maxican pizza in desi style:
  1. Get 1 onion chopped
  2. Prepare 1 tomato chopped
  3. Make ready 1 avocado chopped
  4. Make ready Grated cheese
  5. Take 1 cucumber chopped
  6. Get 2 tbsp taco sauce
  7. Take 1 tsp salsa
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp mayo
  9. Get 1 tbsp ketchup
  10. Take Pinch salt
  11. Prepare Pinch black pepper
  12. Make ready Pinch garam masala
  13. Make ready Pinch chaat masala
  14. Make ready Indian papadam

Instructions to make Maxican pizza in desi style:
  1. Mix all the ingredients well
  2. Now heat up a nonstick pan and at low flame start roasting papad by pressing any cloth gently
  3. Roast both side by turning again and again
  4. Spread some cheese then some mixture then again some cheese as pizza
  5. Set another papad as upper crust and microwave for 15 seconds
  6. Enjoy so yumm maxican pizza at home in desi style
