The ingredients needed to make Pizza dough:
  1. Get 5 cups dough (1 litre)
  2. Get 1 packet instant yeast
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 tbsp salt
  5. Take 4 tbsp oil
  6. Make ready 2 cups luke warm water

Instructions to make Pizza dough:
  1. Take about 5 cups flour in a bowl and create a hole in the centre
  2. Add the yeast, sugar, salt and oil in the centre of the flour
  3. Slowly and gradually add the water to the flour to form a medium hard dough. Dont worry if it's hard since it will soften as the dough rises
  4. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes to make a smooth texture then cover with a plastic
  5. The longer you leave the dough the better it rises when cooking. Best is overnight for better results
  6. Roll out the dough to preferred size and add ingredients as desired
