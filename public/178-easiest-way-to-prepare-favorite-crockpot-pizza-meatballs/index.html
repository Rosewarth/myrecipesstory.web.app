The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Pizza Meatballs:
  1. Make ready 1 bag meatballs(any flavor)
  2. Prepare 1 Jar Spaghetti Sauce (any flavor)
  3. Make ready 1 small bag Pepperoni - 6 oz. (yummy)
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  7. Take 1-8 oz. bag Mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Crockpot Pizza Meatballs:
  1. Place frozen meatballs into crockpot sprayed with cooking spray……
  2. Add your jar of spaghetti sauce….

  4. Add package Pepperoni…..
