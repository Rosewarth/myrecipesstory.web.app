The ingredients needed to make Pizza Mac Salad:
  1. Prepare 1 box large elbow macaroni; blanch and shock
  2. Take 2 1/2 cup pizza sauce/marinara/pasta sauce
  3. Make ready 1 cup pepperoni; chiffonade
  4. Prepare 1 cup cooked pizza sausage; sliced in half
  5. Make ready 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  6. Get 1 cup shredded mozzarella or pizza cheese blend
  7. Take 2 handfuls baby spinach; chiffonade
  8. Make ready 1 large pinch Italian seasoning
  9. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
