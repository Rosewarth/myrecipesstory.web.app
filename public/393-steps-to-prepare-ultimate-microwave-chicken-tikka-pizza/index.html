The ingredients needed to make microwave chicken tikka pizza:
  1. Get for the dough
  2. Take 375 ml warm water
  3. Take 2 tsp dried yeast
  4. Make ready 1 tsp caster sugar
  5. Make ready 4 cup plain flour
  6. Get 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 1/4 cup olive oil
  8. Prepare pizza topping
  9. Get 2 onions
  10. Prepare 2 capsicum
  11. Make ready 6 tbsp tomato ketchup
  12. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  13. Take 500 grams mozzarella cheese
  14. Get 200 grams black olives, if required
  15. Take 1 pinch salt, and pepper

Steps to make microwave chicken tikka pizza:
  1. first prepare the dough. mix warm water, yeast and sugar in a bowl. set aside for 5 min. combine the flour and salt and make a well in centre.add the yeast mixture and oil in it. use your hands to bring the dough to form. knead it for 10 min. then put it into a bowl brushed with oil and cover it with plastic for 30 mins to rise. set aside
  2. heat 1 tbsp of oil and fry onions, and capsicum separately. put the pizza base or dough, after being turned into a base , into microwave for 3 min or until it turns brown.
  3. spread 1 tbsp of tomato ketchup on one side of the base and spread the fried vegetables along with mozzarella cheese. add knns chicken tikka chunks,(easily available in market of any company.)
  4. spread the black olives and sprinkle salt and black pepper.and grill it for 5 min. serve hot.
