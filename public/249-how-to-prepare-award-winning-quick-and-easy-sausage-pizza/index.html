The ingredients needed to make Quick and Easy Sausage Pizza:
  1. Get 2 - ready made pizza crust (with sauce)
  2. Prepare 1-1 lb. hot Italian sausage
  3. Take 1 onion, sliced
  4. Prepare 1 green pepper, sliced
  5. Get Fresh minced garlic (5 cloves)
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup Zesty Italian dressing
  7. Take Sprinkle black pepper
  8. Get 1 can (8 ounce) tomato sauce
  9. Get Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
