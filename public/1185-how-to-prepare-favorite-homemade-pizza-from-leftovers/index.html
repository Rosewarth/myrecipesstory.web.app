Steps to make Homemade Pizza from Leftovers:
  1. For the dough,,,,, mix the warm water slowly in flour and stir it until all the flour are free from lumps and goes firm
  2. Rest the yeast and the dough,, until dough and yeast rise up
  3. Mix the yeast onto the dough and let them rise up, again for 30 mins. then cut the dough in four parts.., knead the dough using by hand, or a machine, or a rolling pin
  4. Pour in some pizza sauce in the center of the dough and spread it out covering the whole dough
  5. Just continue, put all your left over meats,, and veggies, then spread the cheese all over
  6. Bake in a over for 10 minutes
  7. Serves with your favourite cold drinks
