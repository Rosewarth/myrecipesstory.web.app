The ingredients needed to make Satisfying and Delicious Pizza without Cheese:
  1. Prepare Pizza dough for 1 medium sized pizza
  2. Prepare 4-5 tbsp marinara sauce
  3. Get Toppings of choice, (sausages, vegetables, meat, chicken, corn)
  4. Get to taste Salt,
  5. Prepare to taste Black pepper,
  6. Take For white sauce / cheese substitute
  7. Make ready 1 large clove of garlic, finely minced
  8. Make ready 2 tsp vegetable oil
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp butter, softened
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp all purpose flour
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup plain yoghurt
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup water
  13. Take to taste Salt,
  14. Take to taste Black pepper,

Steps to make Satisfying and Delicious Pizza without Cheese:
  1. To make the white sauce, in a medium saucepan over medium low heat, add to the pan, the oil, butter and garlic. Cook that stirring frequently for about 1 minute.
  2. Then, add in the flour and stir to combine, after which add in the yoghurt and water. Cook this mixture for about 2 minutes more stirring frequently and then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Continue stirring until there are no lumps and take off the heat.
  4. To assemble the pizza, roll out the dough to your desired thickness and round it around the edges to get the perfect circle possible. Transfer to a greased cast iron pan or baking tray. After spread the marinara sauce in an even layer over the top of the crust, spreading all the way to the edges.
  5. After,in another layer, spread over the white sauce. (It doesn't matter if a little mixes up with the marinara sauce).
  6. Then, sprinkle or lay over the toppings of your choice. Finally, sprinkle over black pepper and salt.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 400 degrees fahrenheit on the center rack for 15 - 20 minutes, depending on your oven. Happy Pizza Making!
