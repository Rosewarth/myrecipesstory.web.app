The ingredients needed to make Tami's Pesto Pizza:
  1. Get 1 cornmeal
  2. Take 1 ball of premade pizza dough
  3. Take 1 drizzle olive oil
  4. Prepare 2 cup homemade pesto
  5. Prepare 2 packages of fresh mozzarela cheese
  6. Make ready 1 tomato sliced
  7. Make ready 1 salt & pepper to taste

Instructions to make Tami's Pesto Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to highest temp.
  2. Sprinkle a light coating of cornmeal on pizza pan
  3. roll out dough
  4. drizzle olive oil over dough
  5. spread pesto over pizza as the sauce
  6. slice fresh mozzarela put on top of pesto
  7. add sliced tomato
  8. season with salt & pepper
  9. cook for 15 - 20 minutes
  10. slice & eat
