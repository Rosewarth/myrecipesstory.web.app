Instructions to make Quick & Easy Tomato Pesto And Mozzarella Pizza:
  1. Very lightly brush the pizza crust with olive oil. If using Naan bread, I suggest skipping the oil and cooking it well done because it may get soggy.
  2. Spread the pesto over the crust. You can add more or less depending on taste. I use two to three teaspoons depending on the size of the crust
  3. Add half of the desired amount of cheese and slices of tomatoes. Then add the other half of the remaining cheese
  4. Cook as directed on the pizza crust package. I used a Boboli crust for mine this time. It asked for cooking at 450 for 8-10 minutes. I put mine back in for a few more minutes because I like my pizza crispier.
