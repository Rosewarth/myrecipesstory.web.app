Steps to make Steak pizza:
  1. In a medium sized sauce pan over medium high heat, add steak strips.Add in worcestershire sauce and season with salt and pepper to taste. Place a store or homemade pizza crust on a pizza pan with a little flour dusted on the pan.
  2. Continue to saute the steak til it is well done and tender.
  3. Spread the alfredo sauce on the pizza crust and place the steak on top of the alfredo sauce.
  4. Add cheese. Put a 2nd layer of meat and cheese.
  5. Bake at 425 for 45 minutes or til crust is baked through.
