The ingredients needed to make Chicken pizza bread:
  1. Get dough
  2. Make ready 2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Get 1 tbsp milk powder
  4. Take 1 1/2 tbsp active dry yeast
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbsp salt
  6. Take 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  8. Take kneading
  9. Take 1 water
  10. Prepare filling
  11. Prepare 1 cup pizza sauce
  12. Make ready 1 large boneless chicken (chopped)
  13. Make ready 1 large capsicum (chopped)
  14. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
  15. Make ready 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  16. Prepare brushing
  17. Get 1 eggs

Instructions to make Chicken pizza bread:
  1. Mix all the ingredients of the dough and knead a soft dough. Keep in a warm place for an hour.
  2. For the filling: fry the chicken , capsicum and onion in a oil until chicken is tender.
  3. Take it off the flame and add pizza sauce, and cheese.
  4. After an hour, divide the dough into medium sized balls , roll the dough on a plain surface lightly dusted with flour.
  5. In the centre keep the filling and make cuts on both the sides
  6. Now put the cuts on the centre and weave like a basket
  7. Set on a greased baking tray and brush with egg
  8. Keep in the pre heated oven at 200 °c and bake for 10 mins or until golden brown
  9. Enjoy with hot sauce
