The ingredients needed to make Prosciutto and Rocket Filo Pizza:
  1. Get 5 Sheets Filo Pastry
  2. Prepare 6 Slices Prosciutto
  3. Get 6 Cherry Tomatoes
  4. Get 1 Red Onion
  5. Get 1/2 Tube Tomato Puree
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp Creme Fraiche
  7. Take Mozzarella
  8. Make ready Rocket leaves
  9. Get Black Pepper
  10. Take Oil

Instructions to make Prosciutto and Rocket Filo Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 200C. Slice the tomatoes, onion and mozzarella.
  2. Mix creme Fraiche with tomato puree
  3. Lay first filo on baking parchment and coat with melted butter. Repeat step with each sheet, and then roll side to form a crust.
  4. Paste the tomato puree mixture evenly over the base. Next place the mozzarella slices, cherry tomatoes and onion evenly on top.
  5. Sprinkle with black pepper to taste. Cook in oven for 10 mins.
  6. Add rocket leaves and prosciutto before serving and enjoy!
