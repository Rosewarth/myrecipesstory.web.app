Steps to make quick and easy pizza sub sandwiches':
  1. preheat oven to 350º, turn stovetop burner on medium heat and preheat skillet, split Hoagie rolls and butter both halfs of rolls. when skillet is up to temperature toast both halves of the rolls.( I also use my electric griddle to save time when I' m cooking several sandwiches)
  2. when rolls are toasted, spread marinara sauce on both halves of the hoagie roll.
  3. next put slices of ham on one half of the roll, then split a slice of mozzarella cheese in two and put both pieces on top of the ham.
  4. (optional) put mushroom pieces on the on the other half of the roll.
  5. place the open sandwich on a baking sheet and place on the middle rack of the oven. bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and marinara is hot.
  6. Hope you enjoy!
