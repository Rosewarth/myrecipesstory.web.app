Instructions to make Chicken Enchilada Pizza:
  1. preheat oven to 425
  2. bake crust 12-14 minutes
  3. chop onion and jalapeno, add to small bowl. Either with garlic press or by smashing garlic cloves, add garlic to bowl. Add 1/2 cup cheese, taco sauce and drained chicken.
  4. when crust is done baking spread mixture from bowl on pizza taking all they way out to the edge of pizza. sprinkle rest of cheese over top of mixture.
  5. bake 8 minutes
  6. slice tomatoes into quarters and spread out over top of pizza. Add coarsely chopped cilantro.
  7. slice, serve & enjoy! !
