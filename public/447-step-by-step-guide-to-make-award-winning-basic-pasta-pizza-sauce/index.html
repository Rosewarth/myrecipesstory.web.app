The ingredients needed to make Basic Pasta/Pizza Sauce:
  1. Take 1 can 28 oz whole peeled tomatoes (I prefer a good italian brand over Hunts)
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp - 3 Tbl Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  3. Make ready 1 medium onion (I typically use 1/2 large red onion) - diced
  4. Make ready 2 to 3 garlic cloves - chopped or minced
  5. Prepare 1/2 medium carrot (chopped)
  6. Make ready 4 to 6 basil leaves - fresh (may use dried 2 tbls)
  7. Get 1 tbsp tomato paste
  8. Take 1 tbsp dried oregano
  9. Prepare 1 to 2 Bay leaves
  10. Get 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp dry white wine (choose a wine you like)
  12. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste (1-2 tsp each usually)
  13. Make ready 1 tsp + red pepper flakes (optional)
Instructions to make Basic Pasta/Pizza Sauce:
  1. Heat oil in 2-3 qt pot at med-hi. Add onions and garlic. Cook until onions become translucent. (approx 2-3 min)
  2. Add remaining ingredients and bring to boil momentarily then simmer covered. Stir occasionally. (45 min)
  3. Transfer sauce to blender or food processor and blend to desired consistency. (chunky sauce - a few good pulses; blend full speed if preferred smooth)
  4. NOTE Add diced bell peppers or different cheeses for different flavors of sauce
