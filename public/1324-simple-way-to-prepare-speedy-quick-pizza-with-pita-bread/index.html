The ingredients needed to make Quick Pizza with Pita bread:
  1. Get 1 pita bread
  2. Make ready 3 tablespoons pizza sauce
  3. Take 1/4 cup cheese (of your choice)
  4. Prepare 1 sliced onion; medium size
  5. Take 1/4 tsp dried basil
  6. Take 1/2 tsp shredded parmesan
  7. Get 1 tsp tabasco smoked paprika sauce
  8. Get 1 tablespoon I Can't Believe It's Not Butter
  9. Get Optional
  10. Make ready 1/2 packet Papa John's special seasoning
  11. Get 1/2 packet Papa John's red chili flakes
  12. Prepare Substitute
  13. Prepare you can sustitute basil with either fresh basil leaves or Italian seasoning

Steps to make Quick Pizza with Pita bread:
  1. On the bottom side of the Pita bread, apply butter.
  2. Throw it over a pan on low heat. Now, top it with pizza sauce and cheese.
  3. Arrange the onion by pressing it slightly into the bread.
  4. Cover and cook for 5 mins on medium heat. Turn off heat, open it and add Tabasco sauce to it. Finally, sprinkle Basil on top. Also spice it with packets from Papa John's to taste. Serve hot.
  5. Alternatively, you can ready up all this and nuke it in microwave safe plate for 4 minutes (or 1 additional minute if not done).
