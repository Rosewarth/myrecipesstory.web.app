The ingredients needed to make Little pizza shots/mini pizza shots/bread pizza shots:
  1. Prepare - 7 to 8 Bread slice
  2. Make ready Pizza sauce/tomato sauce
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Cheese (Mozzarella/Amul)
  4. Take 2 cup Vegetables crushed (capsicum,cabbage,carrot)
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic chopped
  6. Make ready 1 Onion finely chopped (small) (optional)
  7. Make ready 2 teaspoon Butter/ghee
  8. Take 1 teaspoon Chilli flakes
  9. Get 1 teaspoon Oregano
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper
  11. Prepare Salt as per your taste

Steps to make Little pizza shots/mini pizza shots/bread pizza shots:
  1. Heat the butter/ ghee in a pan.add chopped garlic and saute for a few seconds.
  2. Add onion,saute for one minute and then add capsicum,cabbage, and carrot.mix it well
  3. Now add salt,black pepper,chilli flakes and oregano.mix well and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Now switch off the gas and keep aside the topping.
  5. Now take bread and cut bread with the help of small round cutter or you can use small bowl.
