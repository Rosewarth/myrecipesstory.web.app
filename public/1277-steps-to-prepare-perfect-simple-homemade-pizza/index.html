The ingredients needed to make Simple Homemade Pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 packages wraps
  2. Make ready 1 can Prego Sauce
  3. Get 3 tomatoes
  4. Get 1 can pineapple slices
  5. Get 400 grams ham (any preferred choice)
  6. Prepare 1 brocolli
  7. Prepare 1 packages tortilla chips

Instructions to make Simple Homemade Pizza:
  1. Slice the tomatoes
  2. Slice pineapple slices into smaller slices
  3. Cut ham into strips
  4. Remove stems from brocolli and prepare the leafy portions only
  5. Crush some tortilla chips
  6. Pre-heat oven at 230º
  7. Lay wrap on a plate as pizza base
  8. Apply Prego Sauce on the pizza base
  9. Design the pizza with the prepared tomatoes, pineapples & ham.
  10. Add small portions of brocolli & crushed tortilla chips to finish the design
  11. Place prepared pizza into oven and heat for 6-8min or until brown
