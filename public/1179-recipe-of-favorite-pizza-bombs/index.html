The ingredients needed to make Pizza bombs:
  1. Prepare Pizza dough preparation
  2. Take 2 cups all purpose flour
  3. Get 4 tbsp butter
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp milk powder
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp powder sugar
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp instant yeast
  8. Prepare 1 cup milk (quantity can be changed according to ur dough)
  9. Take Filling
  10. Prepare 125 g Chicken boneless
  11. Prepare Chopped garlic
  12. Get Salt
  13. Prepare Black pepper
  14. Get 1 tsp oil
  15. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  16. Take Chopped capsicum
  17. Prepare Olives
  18. Prepare Pizza sauce
  19. Make ready Mozzarella cheese

Steps to make Pizza bombs:
  1. Preparation of pizza dough:- in a bowl, add all purpose flour, powder sugar, milk powder, salt, butter and yeast. Knead the dough with room temperature milk until smooth. Cover the dough n keep aside for 2 hrs.
  2. In a pan, add oil, chopped garlics. When garlic turn slightly golden brown, add chicken. Cook chicken for 2/3 minutes on medium flame, Then add 1tsp vinegar. add salt and black pepper (to ur taste) n cook until the chicken is soft n completely cooked.
  3. Make a flat round with the dough, just like making a mini pizza. Spread some pizza sauce, add chopped capsicum, olive, mozzarella cheese.hold up 2 side# of the dough and bring all together just in shape of circle.
  5. Greece the baking pan with oil/butter. Place all the pizza bombs on the try, then brush some butter on each bomb, put some mozzarella cheese and olive. N bake for 15/20 minutes, on 375 F or 190C. And then serve mouth watering pizza bombs with some ketchup ! N enjoy üòçü§§ü§§
