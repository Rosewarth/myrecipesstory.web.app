The ingredients needed to make Pizza Dip with Toasted Garlic Baguettes:
  1. Get For the pizza dip
  2. Take 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  3. Get 1 Jar pizza sauce or 2 cup if you don’t like much sauce, just use what you like
  4. Take mozzarella cheese (as much as you like)
  5. Prepare pepperoni sliced or chopped(don’t cut all of it save some for the top)
  6. Get Pizza toppings (I used pepperoni, black olives, any pizza toppings you like on your pizza)
  7. Take toasted garlic baguettes
  8. Prepare 2 baguettes, sliced 1 inch thick
  9. Prepare 5 tablespoons butter, softened
  10. Make ready 2 teaspoons olive oil
  11. Get 3-4 cloves garlic, minced (I used 2 tablespoon of minced garlic)
  12. Take to taste salt and pepper,
  13. Take parsley
  14. Take More pizza toppings ideas
  15. Make ready Pineapple and Canadian bacon, sausage, bacon, onions, peppers, Banana peppers, mushrooms.so many choices
Steps to make Pizza Dip with Toasted Garlic Baguettes:
  1. You can make the dip in the oven, I like to use a deep pie dish when I make it this way, or can be done in a crockpot, if I have time or if I am taking it to a pot luck or a bbq cook out. (oven at 350 for 20 mins or until the cheese melted and is a little brown, crockpot on high for about 2 hrs, till cheese melts)
  2. Mix cream cheese and mozzarella cheese together.
  3. Spread your softened cream cheese evenly across the bottom of your crock pot or pie dish
  4. Pour your pizza sauce evenly on top of your cream cheese
  5. Spread your pepperoni over top, reserving several to top your dish
  6. Sprinkle on your onion, cheese and olives, or whatever toppings you are using (ending with the cheese)
  7. Top with your remaining pepperoni
  8. Meanwhile, prepare the baguettes, as the dip is cooking.
  9. In a small bowl combine softened butter, olive oil, minced garlic, and salt & pepper.
  10. Place sliced baguettes on a nonstick baking pan and spread butter mixture evenly over each baguette.
  11. Sprinkle with parsley.
  12. When pizza dip comes out of the oven, turn the oven to broil and place the baguettes into the oven.
  13. Broil the baguettes until golden. - Serve immediately with the hot pizza dip.
  14. If you are making the dip in a crockpot, I’d just wait till it was done to make the baguettes, it doesn’t take long to make.
  15. You can also sever this with Ritz Crackers, but it would also be good with pita chips or even breadsticks.
  16. Another option is you can add some spices to the cream cheese, ½-1 teaspoon parsley - ½ teaspoon oregano - ¼ teaspoon basil - ¼ teaspoon garlic powder And a handful of cheese just mix all together in a bowl before putting it the crockpot or pie dish or adding some spices to the pizza sauce
  17. There’s so many ways this can be made cheap or all out.
  18. Notes- I use my favorite jar of spaghetti sauce and not pizza sauce, I also make my pizza monkey bread with this and spilt the spaghetti sauce for both recipe, one of the reasons I make them together.
