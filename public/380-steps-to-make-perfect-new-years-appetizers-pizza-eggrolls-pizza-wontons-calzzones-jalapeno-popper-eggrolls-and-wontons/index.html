The ingredients needed to make New Years appetizers pizza eggrolls, pizza wontons ,calzzones jalapeño popper eggrolls and wontons:
  1. Take 1 lb wonton wrappers
  2. Take 1 lb eggroll wrappers
  3. Make ready 1 large pizza dough
  4. Take 1 1/2 lb hamburger meat
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 lb bacon
  6. Make ready 8 oz stick of pepperoni casing removed and sliced thin
  7. Take 1 lb smoked sausage casing removed sliced thinly
  8. Prepare 10 oz cream cheese
  9. Get 8 oz mozzarella cheese shredded
  10. Take 1 large onion chopped
  11. Prepare 2 can tomato sauce fifteen ounces each
  12. Prepare 8 oz can of tomato paste
  13. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
  14. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  15. Get 2 tbsp granulated garlic powder
  16. Get 1 tsp sweet basil
  17. Take 4 oz jalapeños sliced
  18. Make ready 1 oil to deep fry
Instructions to make New Years appetizers pizza eggrolls, pizza wontons ,calzzones jalapeño popper eggrolls and wontons:
  1. in a pan fry your bacon render it well till crisp
  2. sauté the onions in bacon drippings remove when soft add beef half garlic and half salt cook till brown remove cook sausage
  3. for the sauce add sauce ,half the garlic, all the sugar, basil, half salt, heat add paste stir to mix well
  4. take pizza dough half roll out add sauce to the whole dough and other toppings to half of the side of dough fold over the dough tuck and crimp the sides I dont have a working oven did it in a pan on stove top
  5. heat oil. in the eggroll wrappers place ingredients you likke in the center in a diamond way where one corner is straight up amd opposing striaght down fold side corners in to the center then fold bottom up to to and roll the egg roll upwet the corner with a bit of water and stick to side to hold in place deep fry till golden brown
  6. the one I call the jalapeño popper eggroll consist of cream cheese and jalapeño
