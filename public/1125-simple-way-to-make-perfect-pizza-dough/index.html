The ingredients needed to make Pizza Dough:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cup flour
  2. Take 1 tsp sugar
  3. Prepare 1 packages dry active yeast
  4. Prepare 1 cup warm water
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp Olive oil

Instructions to make Pizza Dough:
  1. Put warm water into a bowl with yeast and sugar, mix lightly, let sit for about 5-10 minutes (until thickened)
  2. Mix in flour and salt while mixing (I use a fork), then mix in olive oil
  3. Knead dough about 10 minutes, make it into a ball
  4. Put it in a bowl and let it rise(double in size)
  5. Preheat oven to 425
  6. Roll it out and add toppings
  7. Cook for about 15 minutes
  8. Tip: you can cut the dough into two and roll it out to make two thin crust pizzas, or you can take the dough and stretch making a more traditional rising crust pizza.
  9. Tip: been using the grill lately… Make your dough on a cookie sheet, cook it in the grill on low-medium for about 5 minutes, then put pizza on the grill without cookie sheet for 1-2 minutes. (nice crispy crust)
