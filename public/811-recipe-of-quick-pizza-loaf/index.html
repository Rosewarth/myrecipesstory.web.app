Steps to make Pizza Loaf:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix the ground beef with the parmesan cheese, eggs, and spices.
  3. Make the sauce by combining all the sauce ingredients and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Split the meat mixture into half.
  5. Press half the ground beef mixture into the a pie/cake pan sprayed with nonstick spray. Forming a bowl like shape.
  6. Add a ring of pepperoni around the edges
  7. Add sauce
  8. Add pepperoni and sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  9. Add olives
  10. Add cheese into the bowl part.
  11. Cover with pepperoni
  12. Add the rest of the ground beef mixture to top sealing the bowl.
  13. Cover with sauce
  14. Add cheese and pepperoni
  15. Bake in oven for an hour till done.
  16. Let rest 10 minutes and serve, I hope you enjoy!
  17. This recipe makes one 9"Pizza loaf and one 10" Pizza loaf.
