Steps to make home made pizza from scratch:
  1. For the dough
  2. in a cup put the warm water (40 degree s)and the sugar and 2 tablespoons yeast give it a good stir and cover for 10 mins
  3. if it's too cold the yeast mixture might take longer to proof (form a nice foam on the top) might take 20 minutes
  4. in a bowel mix all remaining dry components together and place the water slowly while stirring. dont put all the water at once as sometines there is humidity in the flower. when a nice ball of dough is formed make a whole in its middle
  5. place the 1 tsp olive oil and knead for 10 minutes or 5 minutes in bread machine
  6. make a ball and place in a clean bowel with a brush of olive oil on the top. cover it and place it in a warm place for 1 hour or until double in size
  7. after it doupled in size cut it into 2 equal parts, give it a gentle knead, form a ball and give it 10 minutes to rest
  8. for the pizza sauce
  9. cut gently through the bottom skin of the tomatos forming a small X
  10. in the boiling water drop the tomatos for 1 min until the skin starts to peal
  11. take out and under cold water peal the tomatos skin and squeeze until seeds are out
  12. give the tomato balls a little blend in your blender with about 1/4 cup of water
  13. in a sauce pan give the garlic and butter 2 minutes together on medium high then pour the tomato mix
  14. add salt, pepper and parsely and stir then lower temprature until you get the texture you love for your sauce
  15. baking the pizza
  16. preheat oven to 425°F
  17. if you use a pizza stone preheat that
  18. take the pizza balls and form your dough on a surface with a mix of flower and little corn flower. "dont make it too thick or it wont bake inside"
  19. I make pan pizza so I bake mine for 3-5 min before placing my toppings
  20. if you use a pizza stone place your sauce and toppings the place for 15-20 min
  21. have fun and enjoy:)
