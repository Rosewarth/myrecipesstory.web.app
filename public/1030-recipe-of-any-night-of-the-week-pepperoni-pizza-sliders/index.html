Instructions to make Pepperoni pizza sliders:
  1. Separate buns and lay on a baking sheet middle side up.
  2. Dab 1t of sauce on each half. A small jar will cover 24 buns or 48 halves.
  3. Lay 3-4 pepperoni on each half.
  4. Top with mozzarella.
  5. Bake 10-12 minuets at 375 until cheese is melted and a slight crisp to the edge of the bread.
