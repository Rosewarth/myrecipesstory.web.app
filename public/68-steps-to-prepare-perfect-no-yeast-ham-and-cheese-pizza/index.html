The ingredients needed to make No Yeast Ham and Cheese Pizza:
  1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  2. Get 3 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 cup water
  5. Get 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  6. Prepare tomato sauce
  7. Make ready quickmelt cheese
  8. Prepare ham
Steps to make No Yeast Ham and Cheese Pizza:
  1. Mix flour, baking powder and salt.
  2. Slowly add water until mixture can form a dough.
  3. Add the oil and knead until there are no more lumps.
  4. Flatten the dough and place in the pan.
  5. Cover with tomato sauce, cheese and ham.
  6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes on 200° celcius.
