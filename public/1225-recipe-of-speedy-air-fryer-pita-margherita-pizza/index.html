The ingredients needed to make Air Fryer Pita Margherita Pizza:
  1. Get Pita bread
  2. Make ready Olive oil
  3. Prepare Pizza sauce homemade or jarred. I like Muir Glen organic
  4. Make ready Optional extra seasoning -oregano, italian and pinch of salt
  5. Get Chopped garlic
  6. Get Chopped onion
  7. Prepare Torn Basil
  8. Take Mozzerella snack balls
  9. Make ready Sliced tomato
  10. Get Shredded cheese of choice

Add your favorite toppings and Cheese. Add a little drizzle of Extra Virgin Olive Oil over top of Pizza. Place in Air Fryer and place a Trivet over Pita Bread. Carefully remove from Air Fryer and cut.

Steps to make Air Fryer Pita Margherita Pizza:
  1. Drizzle and rub olive oil on what will the bottom of your pita bread
  2. Turn over and spread your sauce on with a spoon. Sprinkle with additional seasoning if desired.
  3. Top with garlic, onion and basil.
  4. Flatted out your mozzerella balls(about 6) and place on top.
  5. Top with sliced tomato and shredded cheese.
  6. Place in air fryer and cover with a trivet or something similar so toppings don't fly around
  7. Cook at 350° for 6 mins
  8. If you want a bit crispier crust like me at the end of your 6 mins crank the temp up to 400° and add an additional 3-4 mins
