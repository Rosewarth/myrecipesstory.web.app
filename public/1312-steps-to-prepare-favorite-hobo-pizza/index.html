The ingredients needed to make Hobo Pizza:
  1. Make ready The Crust
  2. Take 3 cups white flour
  3. Get 1/2 cup rye flour
  4. Get 1/3 cup semolina flour
  5. Prepare 1 cup lukewarm water
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon yeast
  7. Take 1 tablespoon sugar
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon sea salt
  9. Take 1 tablespoon olive oil
  10. Prepare The Toppings
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup pizza sauce, warm
  12. Take 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced onions
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  15. Get 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup green peppers

Instructions to make Hobo Pizza:
  1. Pour a cup of lukewarm water and add the sugar make sure it dissolves. Then the yeast, mix well. Let sit for 15-20 min.
  2. Mix all the flour in a large bowl along with the salt.
  3. Pour in yeast/water along with olive oil. Stir and then knead the dough until it is soft yet firm. Keep kneading for 15 min.
  4. Let dough rest and return to bowl. Cover with damp towel. Let rise for an hour or longer.
  5. After dough has risen and is ready. Roll out two equal halves. Each half of dough is enough for two medium sized thin crusts.
  6. Roll out dough on cutting board or table flat. Be sure to sprinkle flour beforehand.
  7. After dough is rolled out flat enough be sure to grease each side with olive oil so they don’t stick to the pizza pan.
  8. Spread pizza sauce and sprinkle with cheese. Add all your toppings and sprinkle more mozzarella.
  9. Pop in the oven and bake at 425 degrees.
