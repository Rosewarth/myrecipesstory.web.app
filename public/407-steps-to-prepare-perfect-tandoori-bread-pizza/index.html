Instructions to make Tandoori Bread Pizza:
  1. Prehat the oven at 350°F
  2. Begin to slice the bacon and ham and drizzle some oil in a pan and cook it until its fully cooked.
  3. Begin to grab your Tandoori bread/Naan and a baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle some extra virgin olive oil onto the Tandoori bread.
  5. Select your favorite toppings. I have choosen some bacon, ham, black olives, tomatoes, spinach, lettuce and feta cheese.
  6. Place your finished pizza into the oven and let it sit for 10 to 15 minutes. Until the sides are golden brown.
