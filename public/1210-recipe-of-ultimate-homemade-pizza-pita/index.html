The ingredients needed to make Homemade Pizza Pita:
  1. Take 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup water
  3. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  4. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  6. Get Ham, hotdogs, green bell pepper, cheese, ketchup tomatoes and mushroom for toppings

Instructions to make Homemade Pizza Pita:
  1. Mix the flour, salt, baking powder and sugar together then pour in the warm water
  2. Fold and knead then let it rest for 15 minutes
  3. Knead and roll the pizza dough for 15 minutes. Then form it into a flat circle then put it in a hot pan. 3 minutes cooking for each sides.
  4. Add the ketchup and your choices of toppings. Made one vegetarian and meat lovers.
  5. Put in oven for about 10 minutes to cook to make the crust crispy! Serve!
