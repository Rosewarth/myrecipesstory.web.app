Steps to make Grilled bread pizza:
  1. Chop onion, tomato, carrot and olives. Mix well.
  2. Boil chicken with salt, garlic, chilly powder and soy sauce and shread this boiled chicken.
  3. Take bread and cut the edges and with the help of a rolling pin roll out the bread into thin flat slices.
  4. Spread some pizza sauce (i already posted the recipe) and on one side of bread place mozzarella cheese, on top add chicken, mixed veggies, again add chicken and cheese.
  5. Roll the bread slice tightly. You can use water to stick edges.
  6. Heat pan, smear some butter, place the bread and press it well and grill until golden brown.
  7. Then flip it and grill other side until cheese melts and become gooey. You can make this in a grill sandwich maker. Grilled bread pizza is ready to be served.
