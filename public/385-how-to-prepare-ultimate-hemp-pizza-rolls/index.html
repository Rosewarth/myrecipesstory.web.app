The ingredients needed to make Hemp Pizza Rolls:
  1. Make ready 420 grams flour
  2. Take 120 grams Durum wheat flour
  3. Prepare 40 grams Hemp Protein powder
  4. Get 11 grams dry yeast or 50 gram fresh yeast
  5. Get 4 grams salt
  6. Take 25 grams local honey
  7. Make ready 15 grams Hemp Oil
  8. Get 10 tomatoes
  9. Prepare 2 onion
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp garlic powder
  11. Take 1 tbsp Nigellaseeds
  12. Prepare 5 tbsp Hemp oil
  13. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  14. Prepare 1 1/2 cup olives
  15. Make ready 1 cup mornaysauce
  16. Prepare 1 bunch fresh spinach leaves

Instructions to make Hemp Pizza Rolls:
  1. Put flour, durum flour, hemp protein powder, salt, yeast and hemp oil in a bowl
  2. Add luke warm water in the bowl and work it till you have a nice light and slightly sticky dough. and sprinkle a little durum flour on the dough and cover the bowl with a wet cloth
  3. Put a pot on the stove and pour hemp oil or canola oil in and set it to high heat
  4. Put the minced onions in and a tablespoon of honey and stir till the honey is melted and add a teaspoon of dry chili
  5. Add garlic powder and Nigellaseeds
  6. Chop the tomatoes in quarters and put them in the pot
  7. Add a pinch of salt
  8. When the tomatoes have boiled out so you have a sauce sample it and add any needed spices
  9. Take a lump of dough and roll it out in a rectangle
  10. Put tomato sauce and your choice of filling on. We opted for onions, san marzano tomatoes, olives, mornaysauce and spinach
  11. Roll it tightly so you get a long pizza roll
  12. Cut the roll into pieces about 1,5 inches thick and place on baking paper on a oven plate
  13. Place in the middle of the oven
  14. When the smell hits you jump with joy for 10 minutes or till the pizza rolls are as golden/brown as you wish
  15. Here comes the hardest part… Now you have to wait till they have cooled enough for you to eat them.
  16. Enjoy ♥
