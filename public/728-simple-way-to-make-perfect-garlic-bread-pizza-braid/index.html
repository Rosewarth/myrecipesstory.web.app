The ingredients needed to make Garlic bread pizza braid:
  1. Take 300 g pizza dough
  2. Get 3 cups grated mozzarella
  3. Get 1 cup chopped pepperoni
  4. Take 1/2 cup butter, melted
  5. Make ready 3 gloves garlic, minced
  6. Get 1 tbsp finely chopped parsley
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup finely grated parmesan cheese
  8. Get Pizza Sauce to dip

Instructions to make Garlic bread pizza braid:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 400F (200C). Divide dough into 9 balls.
  2. In a bowl mix together cheese and pepperoni.
  3. On a floured surface, roll each ball into a tube.  Roll to flatten Ito a long strip about 8"
  4. Add cheese mixture down the middle.  Bring edges up and pinch to seal well.
  5. Place three rolls together and pinch to attach and one end.  Braid dough to form a plait.
  6. In a small bowl mix together butter, garlic and parsley. Brush overtop of plaits and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in oven for 20 minute until golden brown.
