The ingredients needed to make Easy Squeezy Homemade Hand-stretched Pizza:
  1. Make ready Dough
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Hot water (about 105-110 degrees)
  3. Take 2 tbsp Yeast
  4. Make ready 4 cup All-purpose flour
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Sugar
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  8. Get Pizza Sauce
  9. Make ready 2 cup Tomato Sauce
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup Banana Ketchup
  11. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Crushed Basil
  12. Take 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp Crushed black pepper
  14. Prepare 1 Salt and Sugar
  15. Make ready Toppings
  16. Get 1/4 kg Ground beef
  17. Prepare 1 Onion
  18. Make ready 1 Garlic
  19. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Cheese (any cheese you wish)
  20. Prepare 1 Bell pepper (red or green it's up to you)
  21. Make ready 1 Bacon strips or bits
  22. Take 3 Hotdogs
  23. Prepare 1 Salt
Steps to make Easy Squeezy Homemade Hand-stretched Pizza:
  1. First, let's make the dough. In a bowl, mix the water and the yeast. Stir and set aside for 5mins. The mixture should be creamy after 5mins.
  2. Combine the flour, salt, sugar and olive oil. Continue folding through a spatula or you may opt to use a mixer with an attachment for doughs. Continue until all ingredients cohere together and the mixture does not stick to your hands anymore.
  3. This mixture can make 2 doughs so divide it in half.
  4. Prepare two bowls and brush the insides with olive oil.
  5. Stretch the dough and roll. Repeat up to five times then put unto the bowls. Lather the doughs with excess oils in the bowls. Cover the bowls with cling wraps and put in a warm, dry place such as the oven/microwave (turned off) for 2hours.
  6. To make the sauce, combine the tomato sauce, basil, peppers, salt and sugar. You may opt to add ketchup if you wish. Mix well and taste test.
  7. Next is we cook the beef. In a pan over heat, cook the beef with a cup of water. Let it simmer. Wait until there's no more water.
  8. Sauté the ground beef and garlic and onion. When cooked, set aside.
  9. After 2 hours from setting aside, the doughs should look like it increased in size. Use one dough if you're making inmy one pizza and keep the other one in the ref for later use. Knead it with your hands. Roll it. Dust your working area with flour to precent the dough from sticking.
  10. Lay the dough flat in your working area. Make a ridge on the outsides about half an inch and continue pushing with your fingers or knuckles from the center of the dough outwards. The dough should be more flat and bigger by this time.
  11. The dough should be about 7in by this time. Close your fist and put the center of the dough on it. With both your fists, rotate the dough repeatedly. This stretches the dough. Stop until it's stretched and about 9in.
  12. Lay the stretch dough flat again in your working area or a 9in pan. Get the sauce you prepared and spread it across with the back of a spoon.
  13. You may now scatter slices of cheese, or grated cheese, whichever you want, followed by the ground beef, bacon, sliced hotdogs, onions and bell peppers.
  14. On my pizza, i sandwiched the toppings between layers of cheese.
  15. Preheat the oven to 475°F for about 10-15mins. Put the pizza in and bake for about 20mins.
  16. Once 20mins is done. Turn off the oven but leave the pizza for another 5-10mins.
  17. The pizza is now ready!
