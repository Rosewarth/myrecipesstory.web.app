Steps to make Quick Kids Margarita Pizza:
  1. Mix the liquid ingredients together and beat flour in until a smooth batter
  2. Lightly oil the pan on high heat. Pour in the batter and cook for 10minutes
  3. Add the batter and cover. Let it crisp up on the bottom until the sides are crisp and gold making sure not to burn, fry on low heat. Press gently to feel if it's firm.
  4. Flip out the base. Add cheese and tomato base sauce and tomato toppings. Pop in microwave for 5mins on high heat. Slice, let it cool to firm up. Enjoy it warm.
