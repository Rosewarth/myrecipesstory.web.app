The ingredients needed to make Pepperoni pizza pasta bake:
  1. Take 16 oz dry rigatoni pasta
  2. Get 1 can (28 oz) peeled San marzno tomatoes
  3. Prepare 1 can (28 Oz) tomato puree
  4. Get Pepperoni
  5. Prepare Mozzarella shredded cheese divided
  6. Take Black pepper
  7. Make ready Salt
  8. Make ready Basil
  9. Prepare Oregon
  10. Get Onion powder
  11. Get Garlic powder or Minced garlic

Instructions to make Pepperoni pizza pasta bake:
  1. Cook rigatoni’s pasta to box Directions.
