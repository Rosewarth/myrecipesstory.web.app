The ingredients needed to make Chila Pizza:
  1. Make ready 2 cups multi grain atta (flour)
  2. Take 2 tbsp besan
  3. Prepare 1 cup rice atta
  4. Prepare to taste salt
  5. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1 cup water
  7. Prepare to taste finely chopped capsicum
  8. Take to taste finely chopped onion
  9. Prepare to taste crushed paneer
  10. Prepare to taste grated carrot
  11. Get to taste grated Cheddar cheese
  12. Make ready to taste grated Mozarella cheese

Instructions to make Chila Pizza:
  1. Just mix the Besan, Multi-grain atta and rice Atta with salt, red chilli, Ajwain. Then pour water to make a batter.
  2. Put a little oil, pour it over the non- stick tawa, Already pre-heated. After one side is cooked. Turn it over, then apply tomato sauce, put chopped onions, capsicum, crushed paneer, grated carrot and put cheese on top.
  3. Then cover it with an inverted bowl, for the cheese to melt. Once done, take it on a platter and garnish it with oregano and chilli flakes.
