The ingredients needed to make Puff pasty pizza:
  1. Make ready 1 pack ready made puff pastry
  2. Prepare 200 ml tomato pasatta
  3. Get 1 mozzarella pack
  4. Take Half a courgette
  5. Make ready Half a red pepper
  6. Get 1 tomato
  7. Make ready Black olives
  8. Make ready 2 slices Parma ham
  9. Prepare Handful rocket
  10. Get Fresh basil
  11. Prepare Mixed Italian herbs, black pepper

Instructions to make Puff pasty pizza:
  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Roll out the pastry and cut in half. Lightly score about 1cm from the edge.
  3. Simmer the pasatta until it has reduced to half the volume - this takes about 10-15 minutes. Add the Italian herbs and pepper.
  4. Chop up all the vegetables into thin slices
  5. Cover the pastry with pasatta, keeping the 1cm margin free.
  6. Grate the mozzarella onto the pizza bases.
  7. Layer the veg in any combination you want. If using Parma ham or rocket then don‚Äôt add these before cooking.
  8. Cook in the oven for 15 - 20 minutes.
  9. Add Parma ham, rocket and basil once cooked.
