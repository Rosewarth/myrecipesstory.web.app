Steps to make pizza dough - easy:
  1. preheat oven to 200°
  2. put flour and oil in bowl
  3. slowing add water
  4. when dough-like, remove from bowl to lightly floured counter
  5. knead for about 2 mins
  6. cook for 10-15mins until lightly browned
  7. add toppings of choice and bake for further 7-10mins
  8. ENJOY!
