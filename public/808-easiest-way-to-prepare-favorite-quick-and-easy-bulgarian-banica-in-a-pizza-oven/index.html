The ingredients needed to make Quick and easy Bulgarian Banica in a pizza oven:
  1. Take 1 pack thin phyllos
  2. Take 1 yogurt or glass of milk
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 150 grams goat cheese -
  5. Get oil
  6. Get half pack butter
  7. Take 1 glass soda drink

Banitsa (Banica or Banitza) is a traditional Bulgarian food. This dish can be eaten hot or cold, any time of the day, even for breakfast! In many recipes the individual filo "snakes" are placed in an ovenproof dish creating a large spiral. I rolled each filo "snake" into individual spirals and placed them in the dish.

Steps to make Quick and easy Bulgarian Banica in a pizza oven:
  1. Mix in a large bowl the eggs and cheese, along with the yogurt and the soda. Preheat the oven.Put some butter on the oven's pan
  2. Take a list of the phyllos. If they are extra thin take two. Spread it along the pan.
  3. Add some oil with a cooking brush and then add little of the mixture
  4. Repeat step 3 until the phyllos are over. To make it taste better put all the rest of the mixture on top of it.
  5. Put on the cover of the oven and bake for about 20 minutes
  6. Goes well with yogurt drink :-)
