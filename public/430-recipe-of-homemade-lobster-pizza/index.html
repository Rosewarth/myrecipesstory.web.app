  1. Get 1 package pizza dough
  2. Prepare 1/4 lb mozzarella cheese

  4. Take 1 cup ragu
  5. Take 1 tbsp parmesan

Instructions to make Lobster pizza:
  1. Sprinkle flour on pizza pan
  2. Spread pizza dough on pan till desired size
  3. Pour cup of Ragu into middle and spread towards edges of pizza dough
  4. Add lobster
  5. Top with cheese
  6. Let cook in oven at 350 for 20 min
  7. Pull out of oven and sprinkle parmesan cheese and garlic powder(optional) cut with pizza cutter and enjoy
