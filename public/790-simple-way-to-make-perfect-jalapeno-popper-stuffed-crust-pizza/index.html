The ingredients needed to make Jalapeno Popper Stuffed Crust Pizza:
  1. Make ready 2 fresh jalapenos
  2. Get 1 refrigerated pizza crust tube
  3. Get 1 small jar pizza or marinara sauce
  4. Take 1/2 pkg. (4 oz.) cream cheese, softened
  5. Make ready 1 (8 oz.) block Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  6. Prepare 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. Take slices pepperoni
Instructions to make Jalapeno Popper Stuffed Crust Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Chop one jalapeno into fine pieces, seeds removed. Slice the other into strips, seeds removed.
  3. Combine softened cream cheese with chopped jalapenos and 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese.
  4. Example of refrigerated pizza crust.
  5. Open and stretch out over a pizza pan leaving about an inch of crust overhanging pan.
  6. Spoon out cream cheese mixture in a line right inside crust line.
  7. Fold overhanging crust over cheese mixture and make a complete seal with your finger tips.
  8. Top pizza with sauce, remaining Monterey Jack cheese, mozzarella cheese and pepperoni. Brush crust with small amount of olive oil and top with jalapeno strips.
  9. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly. Enjoy!
