Instructions to make Quick & Simple Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cut sub roll in half
  3. Brush or spray olive oil on the top of each slice
  4. Add a lot or a little pizza sauce to top of bread. I like it saucy so I put a lot
  5. Spread cheese over the sauce. Load er' up!!!
  6. Bake in oven for about 10 minutes or until cheese is melty and sides are crispy
  7. Enjoy!!!
