The ingredients needed to make Pizza rolls:
  1. Prepare 1 store bought pizza dough
  2. Make ready 100 ml pizza sauce or tomato sauce
  3. Prepare 250 g sliced ham
  4. Make ready 1 cup grated mozzarella
  5. Take fresh chopped parsley for decor

Steps to make Pizza rolls:
  1. Spread the tomato or pizza sauce on the pizza dough
  2. Cover with the mozzarella
  3. Place the ham slices so that they cover the entire dough
  4. Start to roll the dough with care so the filling doesn't slide off
  5. Cut 1cm slices but be careful not to press the knife too hard in the dough roll
  6. Place the pieces next to eachother on a baking sheet
  7. Place in the oven at 190°C for about 15 min or until it turns golden brown
  8. Spread the fresh parsley in top and serve
  9. You can choose any other toppings but remember to spread them eavenly and not use too many (it gets harder to roll)
  10. Enjoy hot or cold
