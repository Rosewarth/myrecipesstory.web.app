The ingredients needed to make Easy mexican pizza casserole:
  1. Make ready 1 lb hamburger
  2. Get 1 pkg taco seasoning
  3. Take 1 cup salsa
  4. Make ready 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare 4 lg. flour tortillas
  6. Get 1 sm. can refried beans

Steps to make Easy mexican pizza casserole:
  1. Cook hamburger in taco seasoning and drain
  2. Layer each ingredient starting with the tortilla, ending layers with cheese.
  3. Cook at 350° for 30 minutes feeds 3-4 people.
