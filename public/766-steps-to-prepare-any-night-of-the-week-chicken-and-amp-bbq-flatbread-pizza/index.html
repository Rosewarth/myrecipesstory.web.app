The ingredients needed to make Chicken & bbq flatbread pizza:
  1. Make ready 2 Chicken Tenderloins
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup canned corn kernels
  3. Take 1/2 Cup diced canned tomatoes
  4. Get 1 1/2 Cups shredded Monterrey Jack cheese
  5. Prepare 1 Naan flatbread
  6. Take Bbq sauce
  7. Prepare Sour cream

Steps to make Chicken & bbq flatbread pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 450º F
  2. Pan sear tenderloins until cooked, dice into small pieces
  3. Shred 1 cup and a half of monterrey jack cheese
  4. Place the naan flatbread on a baking sheet, spread the shredded cheese onto the naan
  5. Add your diced chicken on the flatbread, do not over crowd.
  6. Add your corn kernels onto the flatbread
  7. Add your tomatoes onto the flatbread
  8. Drizzle bbq sauce around as desired
  9. Place the flatbread into the oven for 6 minutes, leave for 7 to 8 minutes if you want a crispy bottom
  10. Once baked, drizzle sour cream onto flatbread
  11. Cut flatbread as desired, I prefer cutting into small squares.
  12. Enjoy!
