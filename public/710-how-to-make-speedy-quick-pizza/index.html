The ingredients needed to make Quick Pizza:
  1. Get 3 French type of baget bread
  2. Take slices Mozzarella
  3. Prepare I can of crushed tomato
  4. Prepare Oregano
  5. Prepare to taste Thyme, basil, salt
  6. Prepare 2 table spoon vegetable oil
  7. Take Some sharp cheddar cheese or Parmesan
  8. Prepare Handful grape tomatoes
  9. Get 3 cloves garlic
Steps to make Quick Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven at 400 F / 190 C, cut bread set on a oven proof tray. In a small pan mix tomatoes and spices. Add chop garlic and salt. Stir in a medium heat. Until boils
  2. Spread the sauce into spices bread with a spoon. Put in the oven for 10 mints. Than take them and arrange mozzarella cheese over them. Grade some cheddar cheese over mozzarella. And put some mushrooms and other your favorite pizza toppings. I used asparagus and grape tomatoes. Put them back to oven about 25 mints.
  3. Turn the oven off. I added some baby spinach and put them back to oven that you already turned it off, 5-10 mints.
  4. Voila !
