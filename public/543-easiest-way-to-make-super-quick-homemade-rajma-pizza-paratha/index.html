The ingredients needed to make Rajma Pizza Paratha:
  1. Prepare For the stuffing
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  3. Take 1 tbsp butter
  4. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  6. Make ready 1 finely chopped onion
  7. Take 1/2 cup finely chopped capsicum
  8. Get 1 finely chopped tomato
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp coriander cumin powder
  10. Take 1 tsp turmeric
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  12. Take 1 tbsp garam masala
  13. Get 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  14. Prepare to taste Salt
  15. Make ready 1 cup rajma(soaked overnight)
  16. Take as required Water
  17. Get For the dough
  18. Make ready 3 cups wheat flour
  19. Take to taste Salt
  20. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  21. Get 1 tbsp chilli flakes
  22. Take as needed Wheat flour for rolling
  23. Make ready as required Water
  24. Prepare Other ingredients
  25. Make ready as required Butter
  26. Prepare 4-5 tbsp pizza sauce (readily available in the market)
  27. Prepare 1/2 cup grated processed cheese
  28. Prepare 1/2 cup grated mozarella cheese)
  29. Prepare as required Olives
  30. Prepare as required Oil for shallow frying
Steps to make Rajma Pizza Paratha:
  1. For stuffing
  2. Boil rajma for 5-6 whistles and mash it partially.
  3. Heat oil and butter in a pan and put cumin seeds and allow to crackle.
  4. Then add garlic and onion and saute till onions turn translucent.
  5. Then add tomatoes and capsicum and cook for a while.
  6. Then add coriander cumin powder, red chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt and 2 tbsp water and cook for few minutes.
  7. Then add tomato sauce, mashed rajma and mix well.
  8. Cook till the mixture looks little dry.
  9. Off the flame and keep aside.
  10. For the dough
  11. In a vessel put all the ingredients and add enough water and bind medium soft dough and keep aside.
  12. How to proceed
  13. Make small balls of the dough and roll to a roti of about 6 inches diameter of medium thickness.
  14. Then apply butter to half portion of the roti.
  15. Spread pizza sauce on the roti.
  16. Spread the prepared stuffing evenly.
  17. Sprinkle some processed and mozarella cheese.
  18. Put some olives.
  19. Close with the other half.
  20. Press lightly to remove air and seal the edges using fork.
  21. Shallow fry on hot tawa using oil till golden on a medium flame.
  22. Repeat to make more parathas.
  23. Cut and serve with raitas or chutneys.
