The ingredients needed to make The BEST Pizza dough:
  1. Get 4 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  4. Make ready 2-1/2 teaspoons yeast
  5. Take 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon Ground oregano
  8. Get 1/8 teaspoon parsley flakes
  9. Get 1-2 cups Warm water

Steps to make The BEST Pizza dough:
  1. In a Large bowl add Flour, yeast sugar, salt, oil
  2. Combine all with your fingers till it resembles pea size coarse crumbles.
  3. Mix in seasoning
  4. Slowly work in warm water till it forms a soft dough 1/2 cup at a time
  5. Knead on a floured surface till it unsticks to your hands.
  6. Grease a bowl, place dough inside cover with plastic wrap set aside to rise.
  7. Till ready to use. Flatten on greased pizza pan and top with toppings. Enjoy with my pizza sauce recipe on my bio.
  8. Bake @ 350° for 9-12 min
