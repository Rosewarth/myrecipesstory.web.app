Steps to make Sobzee's Pizza Cup Cakes:
  1. Preheat oven to 175 °C
  2. Flour and Grease your cupcake tins well
  3. With a wire whisk mix together the milk, flour, salt, thyme and the egg until well combined
  4. Add the Grates cheese and mix well
  5. Add the Vegetable and sausage and mix well
  6. Laddle into the floured and greased cupcake tins 2/3 ds the way as the mixture will rise
  7. Bake for 20 minutes until they r cooked and golden
  8. Remove from the tins and allow them to cool for 10 minutes on the wire rack, Garnish with pizza sauce and fresh basil leaves
