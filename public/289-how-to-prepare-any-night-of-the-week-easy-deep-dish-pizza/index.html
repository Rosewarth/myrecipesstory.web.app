The ingredients needed to make Easy deep dish pizza:
  1. Get 1 can Biscuits (I used a small can that had 5 for my baking pan. You can use the bigger one depending on size of pan)
  2. Take 1 Pizza sauce
  3. Prepare 1 Mozzarella cheese
  4. Take 1 Pepperoni
  5. Make ready 1 Other toppings you like
  6. Take 1 Italian seasoning
  7. Take 1 Olive oil to drizzle on top

Steps to make Easy deep dish pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Roll out biscuits as flat as you can. Press into greased baking pan. With a fork poke holes in the dough and bake for 10 mins.
  3. When biscuits are done spread pizza sauce, cheese, and pepperoni and whatever other toppings you want.
  4. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning and drizzle with a little olive oil on top
  5. Bake for 10-15 mins until cheese is melted nicely and edges are nice and brown.
  6. Enjoy!
